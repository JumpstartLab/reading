5 Things to look at for poor optimization

Given that this is under 'google analytics' I assume the request is for signs in Analytics that SEO might need address. IE that the site is not optimized to bring the right people's eyeballs in.

  • Low average visit duration
  • High bounce rate
  • Demographics - is it hitting the target regions/demographic sectors
  • Traffic sources - are the words I inted to target the ones people are using to find my site
  • New vs Returning - how is this ratio. A bad ratio = changes needed.
